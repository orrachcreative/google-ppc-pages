The Basics

What Is a Conventional Loan?

A conventional loan is a mortgage not insured or guaranteed by a government agency — offered by private lenders under Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac guidelines. It's the most common home loan in the U.S. — and for good reason. If your credit is solid, you'll typically get lower rates and more flexibility on terms.

Honest Overview

Pros & Cons of Conventional Loans

Before you apply, here's the full picture — the good and the not-so-good.

Benefits

  • Lower rates for borrowers with strong credit (720+)
  • No mortgage insurance with 20%+ down payment
  • Flexible 10–30 year fixed and ARM options
  • Not limited by government loan rules or caps
  • Faster approval — Afordal pre-approves in 24 hrs
  • Works for primary, second homes & investment properties

Drawbacks

  • Typically requires a 620+ credit score to qualify
  • Larger down payment often needed for best rates
  • Stricter debt-to-income (DTI) requirements vs. FHA
  • PMI required if down payment is below 20%
  • Not ideal for borrowers with recent negative credit events
FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before applying for a conventional loan through Afordal.

What credit score do I need for a conventional loan?
Most conventional loans require a minimum credit score of 620. To get the best rates, a score of 740 or higher helps. If your score falls between 620–680, Afordal will help you find programs that keep your upfront costs down.
How much do I need for a down payment?
Conventional loans can require as little as 3% down for first-time homebuyers. Afordal filters listings to match your down payment budget and flags low-to-no down payment programs where they apply.
What is PMI and when can I remove it?
PMI (Private Mortgage Insurance) protects the lender when your down payment is less than 20%. Afordal's client dashboard tracks your equity — so you'll know when you hit 20% and can request PMI cancellation. At 22% it's automatically removed.
How long does the Afordal approval process take?
With Afordal's digital process, you can receive a pre-approval in as little as 24 hours without a hard credit pull. Full loan approval typically takes 14–21 days.
Can I use a conventional loan for an investment property?
Yes — conventional loans work for primary residences, second/vacation homes, and investment properties (1–4 units). Afordal's search can filter specifically for investment properties with financing match overlays, so you see cash flow projections alongside each listing.
What sets Afordal apart from other mortgage platforms?
Most mortgage platforms do one thing — lending or home search, not both. Afordal connects the two, so your pre-approval and your home search stay in sync. You'll know what you can afford before you get attached to a house, and you'll have an actual advisor to call when questions come up.
